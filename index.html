01 — The problem

One vote every four years isn't democracy. It's a suggestion box with a four-year delay.

i.

You vote, then you vanish

You show up, cast a ballot, then wait years while every decision gets made without you in the room.

ii.

They don't actually know you

Representatives guess what you think from polls, donors and lobbyists — not from the people they answer to.

iii.

The issues never stop

Rent, schools, transit, healthcare — decided every single week. Your input shouldn't have an expiration date.
