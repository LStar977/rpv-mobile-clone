## The Problem

Democracy is failing to keep up with the digital age.

- **Low civic engagement**: Only 50% of eligible voters participate in elections; local policy decisions see single-digit engagement
- **Fragmented systems**: Citizens must navigate dozens of disconnected government portals, town halls, and outdated processes
- **Trust deficit**: Digital voting and governance systems lack verifiable identity, enabling manipulation and eroding public trust
- **Opaque policy**: Complex legislation is inaccessible to average citizens—people can't participate in what they don't understand

**The result**: Citizens feel powerless. Policy decisions are made without meaningful public input. Democracy becomes a spectator sport.

---

## The Solution: Represent

**Represent** is the mobile platform that makes democratic participation accessible, verified, and intelligent.

### Verified Participation
- Biometric authentication + third-party identity verification (Veriff)
- Every voice is verified. Every vote is legitimate.

### AI-Powered Policy Analysis (Sentinel)
- Our AI analyzes any law, policy, or regulation against democratic principles
- Provides clear scores on transparency, accountability, privacy, and rights
- Makes complex legislation understandable in seconds

### Multi-Level Governance
- Participate from national policy down to your local neighborhood
- Browse and vote on proposals across transportation, environment, healthcare, education, and more

### Organization Governance
- Any group—nonprofits, communities, enterprises—can conduct secure, transparent decision-making
- Built-in proposal creation, voting, and analytics
