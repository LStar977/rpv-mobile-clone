## The opportunity

Alberta votes in an official 10-question provincial referendum on **October 19, 2026 — 129 days from now.** It is the most-watched provincial vote in a generation, and it has put one question in front of millions of people: *where does Alberta actually stand?*

Every answer available today is unreliable. Online polls, petitions, and social media can't tell a real Albertan from a bot, a duplicate, or someone two provinces away. The largest petition in Canadian history — 386,698 signatures — was dismissed in a single news cycle precisely because no one could prove the signers were real.

Represent is built to solve exactly that. Every user verifies their identity with a government ID. Their location comes from the document, not a checkbox. One person, one vote — and every vote is recorded on a public ledger anyone can audit. Our "Shadow Referendum" is already live: all 10 official ballot questions, in the exact official wording, open only to identity-verified Albertans.

On October 19, when Elections Alberta announces the official result, Represent will hold the only early count built entirely from verified Albertans — checkable against the real outcome, in public, the same night.

This proposal funds a campaign that turns that once-in-a-decade moment into a verified user base, and that user base into a company.

## What's already built

This is not a plan to build a product. The product is live today.

- **iOS app — approved and live on the App Store**, with identity verification, location-gated voting, citizens-only ballots, discussion, and subscriptions
- **Browser voting — live.** Anyone can verify and vote from a shared link, no app install required
- **The Shadow Referendum — running now.** All 10 official referendum questions, verbatim
- **Identity verification — operational.** Government ID plus a liveness check, through a certified provider; we never store the documents
- **Organization tools — built.** Private, verified voting for unions, associations, and municipalities
- **Total running cost: $125/month.** The two founders build and operate everything — no staff, no office, no agencies

Our position is deliberate and fixed: Represent is independent. The Shadow Referendum is an unofficial, non-binding measure of public opinion. We never take a side — our only interest is verified turnout.
